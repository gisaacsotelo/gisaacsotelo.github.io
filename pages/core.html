Identity & Values


RESILIENCE: What is your WHY - how strong is your WHY (Eric Thomas about Bouster Douglas)

QUALITY: Do the things properly or dont do them at all (Dad) - this is to be taken not literally, or you end up doing nothing, he meant the value of your work is given by you, dont do half efforts and put your name on them or that will be the value of your name.

KINDNESS/EMPATHY: The value of a person shows from how they treat the ones that cannot do or offer anythign to them.

INTEGRITY: I am my parents son, and the embassador of their education.

COMMUNITY: No one exists by themselves, no one is self-made, we are all part of the same.

GROWTH: Be a little better than yesterday, and you will never stop moving forward (bad days, weeks, months are allowed, just understand they dont define you be better tomorrow 1% improvements).
